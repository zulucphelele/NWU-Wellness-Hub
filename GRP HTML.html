1. Project Focus: Wellbeing & Student Life

This project focuses on the Wellbeing & Student Life category, as it addresses the everyday challenges experienced by students at North-West University (NWU). The website aims to promote student wellbeing by providing accessible support, practical advice, and useful resources that enhance both academic and personal life.

Many students face challenges such as academic stress, mental health struggles, safety concerns, and balancing daily responsibilities. NWU Wellness Hub seeks to become a reliable digital solution that supports students through these challenges.

2. Website Concept: NWU Wellness Hub

NWU Wellness Hub is a student-centred digital platform specifically designed to support mental health, campus safety, and general student wellbeing. It serves as a safe, supportive, and informative online environment where students can access practical tools, support resources, and guidance tailored to the NWU student experience.

The platform addresses common student challenges such as:

  • Stress and anxiety caused by academic pressure
  • Safety concerns on and around campus
  • Lifestyle management challenges such as budgeting and load-shedding
  • Adjusting to university life, especially for first-year students

By focusing on these key areas, the website provides a meaningful digital support system that enhances both student wellbeing and academic performance.

3. Target Audience

The primary target audience for NWU Wellness Hub includes:

  • First-year students adjusting to university life and campus culture
  • Students experiencing academic pressure, stress, or anxiety
  • Students seeking campus safety awareness and emergency support
  • Any NWU student looking for practical life advice and wellbeing resources

The website is designed to be inclusive, user-friendly, accessible, and easy to navigate for all students regardless of their year of study.

4. Purpose of the Website

The purpose of NWU Wellness Hub is to:

  1. Provide mental health support, coping strategies, and emotional wellness guidance
  2. Promote campus safety awareness through emergency contacts and practical safety advice
  3. Offer student life advice such as budgeting, time management, and load-shedding survival tips
  4. Create a supportive, informative, and empowering online environment for NWU students

Ultimately, the website aims to improve both personal wellbeing and academic success by giving students easy access to support.

5. Key Features

Main Website Sections

  • Mental Health Section: Stress management tips, anxiety coping strategies, and self-care advice
  • Campus Safety Section: Emergency contacts, safety guidelines, and campus awareness information
  • Student Life Section: Practical student advice including budgeting, time management, and lifestyle support
  • Contact / Help Section: Access to student support services and assistance resources

Planned Interactive Features

  • Mood-check feature to help students reflect on their emotional wellbeing
  • Expandable content sections for easy navigation and user engagement
  • Contact form with validation for support and communication

6. Relevance to NWU Student Experience

This project is directly connected to the NWU student experience because it focuses on real challenges faced by students daily. Mental health struggles, campus safety concerns, and adapting to student life are major issues that affect student wellbeing and academic performance.

By providing a centralised platform for support, information, and practical resources, NWU Wellness Hub offers students a digital solution that is both meaningful and relevant.

The website supports NWU’s broader goal of student success by helping students feel safer, healthier, and better equipped to manage university life.
